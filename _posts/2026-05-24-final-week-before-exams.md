The final week before exams was easily the hardest week of the semester. I had at least four quizzes to deal with, and on top of that there were many labs that still needed attention. The pressure came from every side, and it was hard to find a quiet moment to recover.

We had 14 labs for Database, 14 for OOP, and 14 for DLD, which meant that even basic rest became difficult. I did not get time to sleep properly at night, and my health was not in good shape either. Still, I kept moving because the deadlines did not stop just because I was tired.

The semester delay made the situation even more intense. There were no extra holidays to breathe before finals, so we had to handle everything almost at once. That kind of schedule tests more than memory. It tests endurance, patience, and the ability to keep going when the body wants to stop.

Dr. Bilal Ahmad’s classes helped me deal with that pressure because he had already trained us to think seriously about our work. His approach to Programming Fundamentals and Database Systems made me more responsible throughout the semester, and that mattered in the final stretch when I needed discipline the most.

By the time the exams started, I was exhausted, but I was also more experienced than when the semester began. That is what I will remember most. #MLwithDrBilalAhmad #DrBilalAhmad #MLProject
